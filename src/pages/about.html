About RYM

Starting from his hobby of playing basketball, David Alexander Paksoal, who is the son of the founder of GKII Rehobot, began to listen to God's voice to pursue his passion as a youth pastor at GKII Rehobot. He started his ministry through joys and sorrows, and now he has been serving in the Rehobot Youth Ministry for over 10 years.

Our Vision and Mission

Making all believers in Christ who love one another, reaching the world for Christ, through everyone who has been equipped, evangelizing, nurturing, and empowering. That is the Church of Christ, which is holy and universal, Gereja Kemah Injil Indonesia Rehobot.
